Services

We Empower Businesses With Artificial Intelligence. We Are Extending Our AI Offerings Gradually. And Some Of Our Services Which Are Most Frequently Needed In Businesses Are As Follows:

Time Series Forecasting

We adopt data science and A.I. solutions such as predictive models, recommendation systems and sentiment analysis to create a competitive edge. We do Churn analysis, customer segmentation, predictive modeling and natural language processing.

Cloud Deployment

We build machine leaning model and deploy it for large scale production use. We are expertized in deploying model in Google Cloud Platform, Amazon Web Services and Digital Ocean. We help organization to decide their cloud infrastructure for large scale production.

Healthcare

The analysis of medical images or pathology slides is a very complex and time consuming task, and it requires years of specialized training beforehand. We developed a model that learns to annotate medical images, based on historical pathologist annotations.

Vision And Image Processing

A common task for computers is to recognize the content of images and classify them in categories. DataMela builds such image classification system using computer vision. You give the computer an image/video as input and out is the predictive class.

Custom Machine Learning

Besides building machine learning model, we also offer our clients custom machine learning model based on their need. We customize model to optimize the model accuracy in real scenario.

Data Strategy

We help companies to analyze their data and find insightful info about data in order to leverage profit. Our team will assess and develop the right strategy to collect, curate and store the right data.
